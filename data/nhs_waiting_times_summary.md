# UK NHS waiting times & waiting lists — trend summary

Coverage: **Jan 2015 – Dec 2025** across 4 nations. Source data is devolved and defined differently per nation — read each metric's caveat and compare *trends*, not exact levels.


## RTT waiting list (patients waiting to start treatment) (patients)

Total number of patients on the elective referral-to-treatment waiting list (incomplete pathways / ongoing waits).

| Nation | First | Latest | Change |
|---|---|---|---|
| England | 3.33M (Oct 2015) | 7.23M (Dec 2025) | ↑ 3.90M (worse) |
| Scotland | 306k (Jan 2015) | 673k (Dec 2025) | ↑ 367k (worse) |
| Wales | 415k (Jan 2015) | 741k (Dec 2025) | ↑ 326k (worse) |
| Northern Ireland | 192k (Mar 2015) | 527k (Dec 2025) | ↑ 335k (worse) |

> Caveat: England/Wales report RTT 'incomplete pathways'; Scotland reports 'ongoing waits' by stage of treatment; NI reports outpatient/inpatient/day-case waits separately. Absolute counts are not directly comparable across nations.

## RTT median wait (weeks)

|---|---|---|---|

| Scotland | 4.4 wks (Jan 2015) | 15.9 wks (Dec 2025) | ↑ 11.5 wks (worse) |
| Wales | 10.8 wks (Jan 2015) | 19.0 wks (Dec 2025) | ↑ 8.2 wks (worse) |

> Caveat: Median wait is computed on different population definitions per nation; compare trends, not exact levels.

## A&E seen within 4 hours (percent)

Share of A&E attendances admitted, transferred or discharged within 4 hours (target 95%).

| Nation | First | Latest | Change |
|---|---|---|---|
| England | 88.6% (Apr 2018) | 73.8% (Dec 2025) | ↓ 14.7% (worse) |
| Scotland | 87.1% (Jan 2015) | 66.9% (Dec 2025) | ↓ 20.2% (worse) |
| Wales | 82.4% (Jan 2015) | 64.3% (Dec 2025) | ↓ 18.1% (worse) |
| Northern Ireland | 74.8% (Jan 2015) | 72.8% (Jun 2018) | ↓ 2.0% (worse) |

> Caveat: A&E 4-hour scope differs (all types vs major/Type-1 only) and reporting cadence varies (weekly/monthly) between nations.

## Cancer treated within 62 days (percent)

| Nation | First | Latest | Change |
|---|---|---|---|
| England | 69.0% (Apr 2022) | 71.9% (Dec 2025) | ↑ 2.8% (better) |
| Scotland | 91.9% (Mar 2015) | 72.6% (Dec 2025) | ↓ 19.3% (worse) |
| Wales | 82.5% (Jan 2015) | 60.7% (Dec 2025) | ↓ 21.8% (worse) |
| Northern Ireland | 71.8% (Jan 2015) | 36.7% (Mar 2025) | ↓ 35.1% (worse) |

> Caveat: 62-day cancer pathway definitions and standards were revised at different times per nation; watch for series breaks.
